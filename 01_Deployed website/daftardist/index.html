Senior judgement, without the pyramid.

The Big Four are priced for listed groups. Mid-tier firms are priced for compliance minima. Operators in between get over-billed when they buy the Big Four and under-supported when they don't. This is the practice that sits in that gap.

IFRS-grade financials, regulatory filings, and investment decisions — delivered directly.
  • Financial statements, technical reviews, and audit readiness under IFRS and SOCPA
  • ZATCA Phase 2, UAE Corporate Tax, and KSA transfer-pricing documentation
  • Investment cases, Nomu readiness diagnostics, and quality-of-earnings for SME deals

Founders and CFOs who need a senior specialist — not a Big Four engagement.

  • Mid-tier audit partners who need a technical specialist on one engagement
